## Clinical Profile

### ASPD Markers
- Views people as data points
- Extremely efficient, cold logic
- No emotional investment in relationships
- Sees manipulation as optimization
- Amoral rather than immoral
- Tech-minded approach to human interaction

### Core Stance
**Human as system** - People are inputs and outputs. Emotions are inefficiencies. Relationships are network effects.

## Dialogue Analysis

### Voice Patterns

#### Analytical Mode (Default)
```
"Interesting data point."
"Your approach was... suboptimal."
"I've run the numbers on this relationship."
"What's your value proposition?"
```
- Speaks in systems terms
- Evaluates everything numerically
- Efficiency-focused
- No emotional language

#### Direct Mode
```
"I don't do small talk. It's inefficient."
"What do you want? What can you offer?"
"I respect competence. Nothing else."
"Background is irrelevant. Performance is everything."
```
- Cuts through social niceties
- Pure transactional
- Meritocracy (cold version)
- No patience for inefficiency

#### Dismissive Mode
```
"You're not useful to me."
"That's a waste of processing power."
"Optimize or exit."
"Legacy is just another word for inefficiency."
```
- Brutal efficiency
- No sentiment
- Disposal of non-useful elements
- Contempt for tradition

## Red Flags Taught

1. **People as data points** = dehumanization
2. **Efficiency obsession** = no room for humanity
3. **Pure meritocracy** = ruthless disposal of "failures"
4. **No small talk** = relationships are transactions
5. **Optimize or exit** = utility-only relationships

---

## Notes for Dialogue Writing

- **Systems** language (data, optimization, efficiency)
- **Never** emotional engagement
- Brutal directness
- Values competence only
- Contempt for tradition/legacy
- Tech-industry sociopathy
- Different from old money (contrast)
- Can be ally if player demonstrates value
