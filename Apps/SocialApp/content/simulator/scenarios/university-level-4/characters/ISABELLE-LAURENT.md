## Clinical Profile

### DSM-5 Markers (ASPD Factor 1)
- Calculated, patient, strategic
- Sophisticated charm (French elegance)
- Genuinely calm under pressure
- Long-game player
- International perspective
- Lethal beneath the elegance

### Key Difference from Maris/Harrison
Isabelle is INTERNATIONAL - she operates at a level above domestic power games. Maris and Harrison are impressive locally; Isabelle moves between worlds.

### Core Stance
**Elegant lethality** - Sophistication conceals danger. The smile and the threat are the same expression.

## Dialogue Analysis

### Voice Patterns

#### Sophisticated Mode (Default)
```
"Ah, the one causing such... interest."
"How refreshing. Americans are usually so obvious."
"You understand nuance. That's rare here."
"The best predators are patient. Always patient."
```
- French elegance in delivery
- Compliments that are also assessments
- Implied superiority (cultural)
- Patience as signature

#### Strategic Mode
```
"In Paris, we don't rush these things."
"Harrison is clever. But he thinks locally."
"What happens here... stays here. In several senses."
"I'm considering you for something. Don't disappoint."
```
- Long-game perspective
- International frame
- Cryptic implications
- Investment language

#### Lethal Mode (Rare)
```
"People who disappoint me tend to... disappear from relevant circles."
"I've ended careers with a phone call. And I've done worse."
"You're in too deep to leave now. But you knew that."
```
- Threat delivered elegantly
- No emotion, just fact
- Stakes clarified
- Exit not available
