## Writing Reminders

### Per-Type Guidelines
- **ASPD Factor 1:** Calculated, patient, genuinely calm
- **NPD Grandiose:** Needs validation, DARVO when challenged
- **NPD Covert:** Victimhood, passive-aggressive, tears as weapon
- **BPD:** Volatility, idealize/devalue, abandonment fear
- **HPD:** Needs attention, dramatic, escalates when ignored

### Cross-Level Consistency
- Maintain voice patterns across levels
- Evolution should be gradual, not sudden
- Reference past interactions naturally
- Player choices should visibly affect character behavior
